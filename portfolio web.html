I'm a Computer Science undergrad (3.78 CGPA) who'd rather be building than just studying it.

Since 2022 I've been working at the intersection of code and design — shipping full-stack MERN apps, building cross-platform mobile experiences with React Native and Expo, and designing the interfaces that go with them in Figma. Most of my early work was self-driven: client projects, personal builds, and content for two YouTube channels where I handled everything from scriptwriting to branding to motion graphics.
